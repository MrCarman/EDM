Top Decentralized Exchanges to List Your ERC20 Token

We've created a list of decentralized crypto exchanges (also known as DEX) where you can list your custom ERC20 token and offer to sell it for ETH or some other cryptocurrency. Listing your custom ERC20 token to exchanges is one of the next steps you'd want to take after you've created your own Ethereum token.

If you've used our service to create standard ERC20 or mintable ERC20 token you can list your token to these exchanges by yourself since you hold the private keys of the address that deployed ERC20 smart contract.

Top List of Decentralized Exchanges (Updated Daily)

We keep this list updated at all times, with Alexa rank and 24h volume changing every day. We keep an eye on exchanges and will list DEXs on which token owner can list their token for free. If you know about such exchange, contact us so we can add it.
